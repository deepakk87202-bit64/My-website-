WAP-4: Detailed Overview

Dynamic Design

Equipped with 5000 HP, this locomotive is designed for heavy hauling at 140 km/h. Its red livery makes it iconic on Indian tracks.

Operational History

Introduced in 1994 to replace the aging WAM-4, it became the backbone of passenger services in India.

Pantograph System

Features advanced high-reach pantographs for stable current collection at high speeds.

WAP-5: High Speed Legend

Rajdhani Specialist

Specifically imported from ABB for high-speed Shatabdi and Rajdhani express trains.

Lightweight Design

The 5400 HP Bo-Bo design allows for rapid acceleration up to 160 km/h.

Aerodynamic Body

The sloped front helps in cutting through air resistance during high-speed runs.

WAP-7: The King of Tracks

Extreme Power

Generating 6350 HP, it can pull 24-coach trains with ease at 140 km/h.

Regenerative Braking

Highly efficient energy-saving technology that feeds power back to the grid during braking.

Reliability

Known for its rock-solid reliability across all railway zones in India.
