Mission 100% Electrification

Indian Railways is on track to become the world's largest 100% electrified broad-gauge network, bringing down carbon emissions massively.

The Diesel Legends

Even as electrification expands, diesel workhorses like the WDP-4D remain crucial for strategic routes, unelectrified zones, and emergency hauling.

Regenerative Braking

Modern electric locos like WAP-5 and WAP-7 use 3-phase AC technology allowing them to generate electricity and feed it back into the grid when braking!
