WAP-4 Series

Legendary Build

Manufactured by CLW, the WAP-4 is a more powerful version of WAP-1, featuring a lighter body and high-speed pantographs.

Performance

Known for its distinctive 'whine' and red livery, it remains the backbone of Indian Railways' passenger fleet.

WAP-5 Series

Swiss Technology

Based on the ABB Locomotive, the WAP-5 features regenerative braking and GTO/IGBT traction control.

Speed Mastery

Primarily used for Gatimaan and Bhopal Shatabdi, it is built for the fastest tracks in India.

WAP-7 Series

The Heavy Hauler

Equipped with 3-Phase technology, it is the most powerful passenger locomotive in Indian Railways' regular service.

Energy Efficiency

WAP-7 uses Hotel Load technology to provide power to train coaches directly from the loco.

WDP-4D Series

Dual Cab Design

The 'D' stands for Dual Cab, resolving the visibility issues of its predecessors while maintaining the 4500 HP output.

Diesel Dominance

Powered by the EMD 710 engine, it is renowned for its reliability on challenging terrains and long-distance diesel hauls.
